YouTuber James Charles Announces Beautiful Collaboration With Morphe

YouTube sensation James Charles, the first male CoverGirl brand ambassador, will launch his first makeup collection. The beauty expert announced the collaboration with Morphe on his channel, which has over 9.4 million subscribers, with the hashtag #UnleashYourInnerArtist. Last year, he walked the runway for MarcoMarco's Six 1/2 collection during Los Angeles Fashion Week. The collection, named the Sister Collection, after his pet name for fans who he calls sisters, includes a 39-shade eyeshadow palette, $39, and a set of 34 brushes and a blending sponge, $149. Charles took the chance to apply some of the colors in a demonstration video and debuted a commercial that showcases the many stellar looks you can achieve with the palette. The collection will be available on November 13 on the Morphe website.
